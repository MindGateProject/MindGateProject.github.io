Project Stargate Timeline

Initial Timeline

1950s - 1960s: Project Bluebird and Project Artichoke

  • Project Bluebird (1950-1953): Investigated techniques for interrogation and mind control.
  • Project Artichoke (1951-1956): Focused on hypnosis, drugs, and other methods for interrogation and behavior modification.

1960s - 1970s: MKUltra

  • MKUltra (1953-1973): A broad program involving research on behavioral modification and mind control, including the study of LSD and other psychoactive substances.

1970s - 1980s: Project Scanate and Project Grill Flame

  • Project Scanate (1970s): A precursor to Project Stargate, exploring psychic phenomena and remote viewing.
  • Project Grill Flame (1978-1983): Investigated remote viewing for intelligence gathering purposes, leading to the formal establishment of Project Stargate.

1980s - 1990s: Project Stargate and its Spin-Offs

  • Project Stargate (1983-1995): The formalized remote viewing program conducted by the CIA and DIA.
  • Project Center Lane (1985-1995): Focused on the remote viewing of specific individuals or objects.
  • Project Sun Streak (1987-1995): Investigated remote viewing for intelligence purposes.
  • Project Grill Flame II (1983-1991): An extension of Project Grill Flame, refining remote viewing techniques.

1990s - Present: Declassification and Aftermath

  • Declassification (1995-2017): Documents related to Project Stargate and related projects were declassified and made public.
  • Continued Interest: Interest in psychic phenomena and remote viewing persists.

Expanded Timeline

1950s - 1960s: Project Bluebird and Project Artichoke

  • Project Bluebird (1950-1953): Investigated techniques for interrogation and mind control, including hypnosis, drugs, and other methods.
  • Project Artichoke (1951-1956): Focused on hypnosis, drugs, and other methods for interrogation and behavior modification, often using unwitting subjects.

1960s - 1970s: MKUltra

  • MKUltra (1953-1973): A broad program involving research on behavioral modification and mind control, including the study of LSD and other psychoactive substances. This project encompassed numerous sub-projects and experiments.

1970s - 1980s: Project Scanate and Project Grill Flame

  • Project Scanate (1970s): A precursor to Project Stargate, exploring psychic phenomena and remote viewing, often conducted by independent researchers.
  • Project Grill Flame (1978-1983): Investigated remote viewing for intelligence gathering purposes, leading to the formal establishment of Project Stargate.

1980s - 1990s: Project Stargate and its Spin-Offs

  • Project Stargate (1983-1995): The formalized remote viewing program conducted by the CIA and DIA, involving psychic individuals known as "remote viewers" tasked with accessing information about remote locations and targets.
  • Project Center Lane (1985-1995): Focused on the remote viewing of specific individuals or objects, often with military or intelligence applications.
  • Project Sun Streak (1987-1995): Investigated remote viewing for intelligence purposes, particularly targeting foreign military installations and activities.
  • Project Grill Flame II (1983-1991): An extension of Project Grill Flame, refining remote viewing techniques and exploring additional applications.
